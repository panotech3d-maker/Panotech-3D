FAQ

Common questions about virtual tours.

What is a 360° Virtual Tour?

A 360° Virtual Tour is an interactive online experience where users can explore a property or business as if they are physically present.

What is a Google Business Virtual Tour?

It is a 360° walkthrough published on Google Search and Google Maps, helping customers explore your business online.

Can I add the tour to my website?

Yes, Kuula virtual tours can be embedded directly into your website and shared through social media, email and QR codes.

How much does a virtual tour cost?

Pricing depends on property size, number of 360° images, location and client requirements. Contact us for a customized quote.
