FAQ

What is a 360° Virtual Tour?

A 360° Virtual Tour allows customers to explore a business or property online with panoramic views and interactive navigation.

What is a Google Business Virtual Tour?

It is a 360° walkthrough published on Google Search and Google Maps, helping customers explore your business before visiting.

Can the tour be added to my website?

Yes, Kuula and Google Business tours can be embedded on your website and shared on WhatsApp, social media and email.

Which businesses need virtual tours?

Real estate, offices, restaurants, hotels, clinics, schools, factories, showrooms and event spaces can benefit from virtual tours.
