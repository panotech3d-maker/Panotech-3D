Virtual Tour FAQ

Common questions from Ahmedabad businesses before starting a 360° virtual tour.

What is a 360° Virtual Tour?

A 360° Virtual Tour is an interactive digital walkthrough that allows customers to explore your space online from mobile, desktop or tablet.

What is a Google Business Virtual Tour?

It is a 360° tour published on Google Search and Google Maps, helping visitors explore your business before they visit.

Which businesses in Ahmedabad can use virtual tours?

Real estate, offices, restaurants, hotels, schools, hospitals, showrooms, banquet halls and industrial spaces can benefit from virtual tours.

How long does delivery take?

Timeline depends on the project size. Most small and medium projects can be delivered within a few business days after capture.

Can the virtual tour be added to my website?

Yes, Kuula and Google virtual tours can be embedded or linked on your website and shared through marketing channels.
